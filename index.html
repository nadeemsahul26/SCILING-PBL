Every DAY Science Vocabulary

Myth-Busting Science Vocabulary: Understanding Beyond Words

This website focuses on enhancing peoples’ science vocabulary by connecting it to everyday life. Few common scientific terms or concepts will be selected, creating some engaging and informative materials that help people understand and use these terms in their daily conversations.

  • What you'll achieve by this book

    This website aims to bridge the gap between scientific language and everyday communication, helping the audience better understand and use science-related terms in their daily lives. It's a practical way to improve science literacy while enhancing language skills.

  • Selection of Science Terms

    Choosing a set of scientific terms or concepts that are frequently used in everyday life but might not be fully understood.
